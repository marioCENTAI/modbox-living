Why We Exist

Container housing worked in Amsterdam. It can work in Limassol.

In the Netherlands, Keetwonen housed 1,000 students across 12 five-storey container buildings. In South Africa, Drivelines Studios created 140 apartments from stacked containers in downtown Johannesburg. In the US, Phoenix saw the first market-rate container apartment complex rent units below the city average.

The model is proven. Container architecture isn't a compromise — it's a smarter way to build. Faster, greener, and significantly cheaper than conventional construction. We're bringing it to Cyprus.

The Housing Crisis in Cyprus

Limassol has become unaffordable for the people who live and work here.

The surge of international businesses, tech companies, and high-net-worth relocators has pushed rental prices beyond the reach of local professionals, young families, and working expats. The city grew faster than its housing supply. Something has to change.
